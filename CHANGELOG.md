# Changelog

## 0.1.0

Initial release.

- Chapter-level reading progress tracking via TOC
- Item Pane section with progress bar, chapter tree, cascading checkboxes
- Item list progress column (`3/7` format)
- Auto-detection of active chapter from reader position
- Click-to-navigate from chapter list to reader
- Support for PDF and EPUB attachments
- Data stored in parent item Extra field (`zp-read:` lines)
